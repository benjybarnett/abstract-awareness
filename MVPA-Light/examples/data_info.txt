Datasets taken from the free BCI data repository http://bnci-horizon-2020.eu/database/data-sets
For more information on the dataset, see http://doc.ml.tu-berlin.de/bbci/BNCIHorizon2020-MusicBCI/BNCI_MusicBCI.pdf and Treder et al. (2014).

Data correspond to subjects VPaak, VPaan, VPgcc
The "SynthPop" condition has been selected with attended and unattended deviants. The 64 channels have been reduced to 32 channels.

Reference: 
MS Treder, H Purwins, D Miklody, I Sturm, B Blankertz (2014).
Decoding auditory attention to instruments in polyphonic music using single-trial EEG classification.
Journal of neural engineering 11 (2), 026009
